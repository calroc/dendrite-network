Dendrite Network

  • Consumers get good products and the warmth of knowing their purchases help the community
  • Vendors get rapid access to effectively all potential market/customers
  • All Participants get a zero-risk zero-cost income-creating game to play

The economic facts of life are changing.

We're creating a network of relationships and economic exchanges to go along with this transformation. It will allow us to surf the transition with ease and comfort.

The Dendrite Network is something that's already happening.

We're just making it explicit and bringing it into conscious awareness.

How It Works

Consumers

Get timely offers from people you know.

Provide feedback (positive, negative, comments) directly to vendors.

Make purchases.

Some of the amount of your purchases goes to people in your network.

Participants

Pass offers on to other people you know.

Collect propagation fees from purchases made by people in your network.

No Risk

No Cost

Vendors

Reach your customers quickly through friendly word-of-mouth.

Get direct response to your offers and products.

Complete control of costs.

Pay only for actual sales.
